311 Neighborhood Equity – Interactive Figures

Interactive maps and visualizations created for the 311 Neighborhood Equity project, examining spatial, temporal, and socioeconomic patterns in San Francisco 311 service requests.

  • ACS Discovery Map
    Exploratory map of American Community Survey indicators used to contextualize neighborhood conditions.
  • 311 Requests by Census Tract
    Choropleth map showing spatial variation in 311 request volume across San Francisco census tracts.
  • 311 Request Source Map
    Geographic distribution of 311 requests by submission channel and reporting source.
  • San Francisco 311 Tract Map
    Base census tract geography used for spatial joins and equity-focused analysis.
  • 311 Service Request Timeline
    Temporal visualization showing trends in 311 service requests over time across San Francisco.
  • Data Cleaning & QA Map
    Visualization documenting spatial filtering, data cleaning decisions, and quality assurance steps.
